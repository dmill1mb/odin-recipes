Baked Ham and Cheese Sliders

Why should you make this?

This recipe is a fun and unique take on classic.This also perfect for the picky eater in the family. alright lets gets started.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup of melted butter
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons poppy seeds
  • 1 tablespoon dried minced onion
  • 24 mini sandwich rolls or 12 large
  • 1 pound thinly sliced cooked deli ham
  • 1 pound thinly sliced swiss cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F then grease a 9 by 13-inch baking dish.
  2. Mix butter,mustard,worcestershire sauce,poppy seeds,and dried onion in a bowl
  3. split the rolls and put the bottoms on the pan.Then put half of the ham on the rolls. Next and the swiss cheese and final put the rest of the ham on the roll
  4. place the top half on the roll on top then pour the sauce over the rolls
  5. Baked in the oven for about 20 minutes or until the rolls are light brown then you ready to serve
