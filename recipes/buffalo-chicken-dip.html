Buffalo Chicken Dip

Why should you try this

I'm not calling this the best dip in the world because it is my favorite. I say it is the best dip because my whole family love it,but enough playing It's a simple recipe and have the right amount of spice for everybody. Now lets get ready to make a masterpiece

Ingredients

  • 20 ounce can of chunk chicken, drained
  • 3/4 cup hot pepper sauce
  • 16 ounce of package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup ranch dressing
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 bunch celery
  • 8 ounce box chicken flavored crackers or whatever crackers you want

Step

  1. Heat chicken and hot pepper asuce in a skiller over medium heat until heated throught. stir in cream cheese and ranch dressing.Cook and stir until well blended and warm
  2. Mix in 1/2 of the cheddar cheese and transfer the mixture to a slow cooker. Sprinkle the remaing cheese over top
  3. Cover and cook on low until dip is hot and bubbly
  4. now You ready to serve
